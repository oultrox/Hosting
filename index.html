About Me

Hi! I'm Jesús Pérez! I'm a Senior Gameplay Engineer with 10 years of experience in development and design of different video games using a variety of game engines such as Unity3D, Construct e, developed prototypes and game systems in Godot and Unreal engine 4 as well. 

I'm always eager to dive into new challenges and learn as much as I can. Creating and exploring in the world of gaming brings me immense joy, and I'm constantly driven to expand my skills and knowledge.

I love to play MMORPGs, Shooters, and single RPG games as well. Guild Wars 2, FFXIV, Metal Gear Solid Series and XDefiant are my jams!
